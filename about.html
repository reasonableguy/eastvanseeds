About East Van Seeds

Welcome to the East Van Seed Library

Our Seed Library is where gardening and community unite. We see gardening as more than a hobby; it’s a deep connection to nature’s beauty, nurturing life from seeds. Our mission is simple yet profound: to cultivate a thriving community through seeds.

Why We’re Starting The Seed Library

  • Diverse Seeds: Our library safeguards genetic diversity, housing a wide variety of seeds for future generations.

  • Local Partnerships: We collaborate with local seed libraries and organizations, bolstering efforts to preserve biodiversity.

  • Gardening Resource Hub: Beyond seeds, we offer expert tips, articles, and guides for all levels of gardeners.

  • Monthly Guide: Our monthly guide helps you make the most of each season.

Join Us in Cultivating Beauty and Community

Join Our Seed Library to nurture both gardens and community. Whether you’re a seasoned gardener or a beginner, we’re here to support you, celebrate nature’s wonders, and sow the seeds of a greener future.
