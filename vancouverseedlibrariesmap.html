Vancouver climate

Average Frost Dates:

First Avg Frost: Nov 2

Last Avg Frost: March 28

You often see packets say "plant 6 weeks before last/first frost date". This is what they are referring to.

Vancouver Plant Hardiness Zone: 8b

(some surrounding areas go up to 9).
Look for plants or seeds that are meant for zone 8b to ensure the best success.
